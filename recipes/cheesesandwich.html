Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

Welcome to our grilled cheese sandwich recipe! Nothing beats the comfort and simplicity of a classic grilled cheese sandwich. This recipe takes the traditional grilled cheese to the next level with the addition of a few key ingredients.

This grilled cheese sandwich is perfect for a quick and easy lunch, or a comforting dinner on a cold night. It's a crowd-pleaser that will have everyone asking for seconds. So, fire up your stove and get ready to enjoy the ultimate grilled cheese sandwich!

Ingredients

  • 4 slices white bread
  • 3 tablespoons butter, divided
  • 2 slices Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Generously butter one side of a slice of bread. Place bread butter-side down in the hot skillet; add 1 slice of cheese. Butter a second slice of bread on one side and place butter-side up on top of cheese.
  2. Cook until lightly browned on one side; flip over and continue cooking until cheese is melted. Repeat with remaining 2 slices of bread, butter, and slice of cheese.
